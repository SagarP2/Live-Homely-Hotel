About LIVEHOMELY

We're revolutionizing the hotel booking experience with seamless technology and exceptional service.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Live homely began with a simple mission: to create a hotel reservation system that combines technological innovation with a personal touch. Our founders, experienced hoteliers and tech enthusiasts, recognized the need for a solution that serves both hotel managers and their guests.

What started as a small startup with just five team members has now grown into a comprehensive platform serving over 500 hotels worldwide. Throughout our journey, we've remained committed to our core values of excellence, innovation, and customer-centricity.

Our system was designed from the ground up to address the real challenges faced by hotels of all sizes. By working closely with industry professionals, we've developed features that streamline operations, increase bookings, and enhance the guest experience.

Today, Luxe Reservations stands as a testament to what happens when hospitality expertise meets cutting-edge technology. We continue to evolve our platform based on user feedback and industry trends, ensuring our clients always stay ahead of the curve.

What Sets Us Apart

Our reservation system combines powerful features with elegant simplicity. We've eliminated the complexity typically associated with hotel management software, while retaining all the functionality needed to run a successful property.

From seamless channel management to intuitive booking interfaces, every aspect of our platform is designed to save time, increase revenue, and enhance the guest experience.

Unlike other systems that require extensive training, Luxe Reservations can be implemented and mastered in days, not weeks. Our cloud-based approach means no expensive hardware or complicated installations.
